Ingredients

  • 1 cup Butter or magarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup Brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown sugar, packed
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon Flour,unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon Baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup Walnuts or peans, chopped (optional)
  • 2 cups KCC Bittersweet 60% Cacao Baking Chips

Baking instructions

  • Heat oven to 500F.
  • Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set aside.
  • In large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar, and brown sugar at medium sped until creamy and lightened in color.
  • Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time. Mix on low speed until incorporated
  • Gradually blend dry mixture. Stir in nuts and chocolate chips
  • Drop by tablespoon unto ungreased cookie sheets.
  • Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or untill chocolate chip cookies are golden brown.
  • Enjoy
